# SMB AI Workflow Audit — Draft Offer Options

> Draft internal planning note. This is not a live public offer, not pricing advice, not a financial guarantee, and not approval to sell, publish, invoice, or contact anyone. Any real offer, price, outreach, payment setup, or delivery work requires Charlie's explicit approval first.

## Pricing principles

- Sell a bounded decision-support deliverable, not vague "AI transformation".
- Keep scope small enough to deliver safely without private system access by default.
- Do not promise revenue, savings, compliance, accuracy, or ROI.
- Prefer fictional, redacted, or manually supplied examples until a written permission plan exists.
- Make every package include human review, non-goals, risk notes, rollback/deactivation steps, and next actions.

## Option A — Starter Workflow Audit

- Draft price range for later review: £250–£400 fixed fee.
- Scope: one 30–45 minute discovery call, 3–5 workflows mapped, 1 recommended draft-only pilot.
- Deliverable: short report using `templates/audit-report-template.md`.
- Best for: owner-operated businesses that want a low-commitment first look.
- Not included: implementation, live system access, customer-message automation, compliance review, or guaranteed savings.

## Option B — Standard Audit + Pilot Blueprint

- Draft price range for later review: £600–£900 fixed fee.
- Scope: discovery call, 5–8 workflows mapped, top 3 opportunity shortlist, and one detailed human-reviewed pilot blueprint.
- Deliverable: completed audit report plus pilot checklist, prompt/checklist draft, risk controls, and rollback plan.
- Best for: businesses that already feel admin pain and want a concrete next step without granting broad access.
- Not included: live integrations, ongoing support, payment/accounting write access, or autonomous external messaging.

## Option C — Advisory Review Add-on

- Draft price range for later review: £150–£300 add-on.
- Scope: one follow-up review call after the report to decide whether to stop, refine, or approve a separate implementation plan.
- Deliverable: updated next-action list and implementation readiness notes.
- Best for: owners who need help prioritizing safely after reading the report.
- Not included: implementation, procurement, vendor account creation, or production deployment.
